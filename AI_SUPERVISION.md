# AI Supervision Rules for Automated Agents

## Purpose
The AI supervision rules aim to provide guidelines for the ethical and responsible use of automated agents.

## Key Principles
1. **Transparency**: Users should be informed about when they are interacting with AI.
2. **Accountability**: Clear accountability frameworks should be established for the actions of automated agents.
3. **Safety**: Measures must be taken to ensure the safety of automated agents and the environments in which they operate.
4. **Privacy**: Respect user privacy in the design and implementation of automated systems.
5. **Fairness**: Ensure that automated agents operate fairly without discrimination.

## Implementation
- All automated agents must undergo regular audits to ensure compliance with these rules.
- Users must have clear ways to report issues and concerns regarding automated interactions.

This document should be reviewed on a quarterly basis to ensure its relevance and effectiveness.
